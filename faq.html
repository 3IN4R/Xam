Where can I find your products?

If you reside in Denmark, you can order our teas by delivery from Nemlig or you could buy physically from stores such as LIDL, Netto and Rema 1000

How much can I drink a day?

We recommend around 3 to 5 cups of tea a day. If you are a dark tea drinker, then we would not recommend consuming it at the evenings af it could affect your sleep quality.
