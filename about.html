The Story of Pickwick

Our story begins in 1753 with a Dutch tea, coffee and smoke retailer named Egbert Douwe. For many years our brand has been under the name of Egbert Douwe, but after a rise in popularity in our English style packaging we have decided to rebrand ourselves into Pickwick. We offer high-quality teas for every taste and believe that our products can improve your life, regardless of your lifestyle.

Social Responsibility

We care about the future. We are part of the Ethical Tea Partnership, which ensures that our products are made with the utmost of care for our workers and the environment.
